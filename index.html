Concealment Layers for Online Anonymity and Knowledge (CLOAK) is the first publicly available knowledge base on cybercriminal concealment measures. CLOAK has been developed by analyzing many Operational Security (OpSec) guides from both the clear- and darkweb. Its main objective is to combat cybercrime better and support the CTI community.
